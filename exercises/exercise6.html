About Me

Ella Desmond is a senior Journalism major with a Public Relations concentration at the University of Massachsuetts Amherst.

A little bit more about me

Ella is also pursuing a Professional Writing and Technical Communication Certificate. This will provide her with extra skills for digital communication and technical documentation.

Ella is the Public Relations Director for the UMass Chapter of the Public Relations Student Society of America (PRSSA). In this position, she manages the chapter's presence both online and within the UMass community. She also oversees the chapter's Public Relations committee, comprised of a social media manager, website manager, and blog manager.

After graduation, Ella plans to pursue a career in campaign management, and hopefully speech writing.
