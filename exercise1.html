Ella Desmond's longer bio

Ella Desmond is a senior Journalism major with a Public Relations concentration at the University of Massachsuetts Amherst. She is also pursuing a Professional Writing and Technical Communication (PWTC) certificate.

Ella is the Public Relations Director for the UMass Chapter of the Public Relations Student Society of America (PRSSA). In this position, she manages the chapter's presence both online and within the UMass community. She also oversees the chapter's Public Relations committee, comprised of a social media manager, website manager, and blog manager.

After graduation, Ella plans to pursue a career in campaign management, and hopefully speech wriitng.
